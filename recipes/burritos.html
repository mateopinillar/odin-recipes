Burritos

Description

This is a recipe for a Tex-Mex chicken burrito. We will be using a wide variety of ingredients. We will start by first making the guacamole. Keep in mind it is not Gluten free.

Ingredients

  • Flour tortilla
  • Avocados
  • Lime
  • Cilantro
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Taco seasoning
  • Chicken
  • Cheese
  • Sour cream
  • Refried beans
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Take 3 avocados and scoop the flesh into a bowl
  2. Smash them together
  3. Add salt, pepper, and a little chili powder
  4. Start cutting and dicing your tomatoes and onions
  5. Add everything to the avocado bowl
  6. Squeeze half a lime into the bowl
  7. Mix everything and garnish with some Cilantro
  8. Cut the chicken into pieces and add some oil and taco seasoning
  9. While cooking make the rice
  10. Add to a pan with more oil
  11. Warm up tortilla with the same pan
  12. Then assemble with some rice, beans, chicken, cheese, sourcream, and our guacamole.
  13. And Enjoy!
