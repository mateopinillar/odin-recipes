Smash-Burger

In this recipe we will be making the famous smash burgers. Very similar to a regular burger. This recipe is the way I personally like making burgers.

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • American Cheese
  • Onions
  • Pickles
  • Burger Patties
  • Ketchup
  • Mayonaise
  • French Fries
  • Salt and pepper
  • Onion powder
  • Paprika
  • Garlic Powder

Steps

  1. Heat the pan
  2. Form beef balls
  3. Toast the buns
  4. Smash the burgers
  5. Add salt and pepper
  6. Flip the burger
  7. Add cheese
  8. Air fry french fries
  9. Assemble burger
