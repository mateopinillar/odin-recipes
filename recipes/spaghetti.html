Spaghetti

Description

This is a recipe for classic spaghetti and meatballs. Should be not to difficult to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1/2 cup of breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley (or 1 tbsp dried)
  • 1/2tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • 1/2 tbsp olive oil (for frying)
  • 12 oz (340g) spaghetti
  • Salt (for pasta water)
  • Fresh basil or extra Parmesan (optional, for garnish)

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine ground meat, breadcrumbs, Parmesan, egg, garlic, parsley, salt, pepper, and milk.
  2. Mix gently (don't overwork the meat).
  3. Roll into 1-1/2 inch balls.
  4. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  5. Brown the meatballs on all sides (about 6-8 minutes total). They don't need to be fully cooked yet.
  6. Remove and set aside.
  7. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  8. Add spaghetti and cook according to package instructions until al dente.
  9. Drain (reserve 1/2 cup pasta water if needed).
  10. Toss spaghetti with some sauce (add a splash of pasta water if needed).
  11. Plate and top with meatballs and extra sauce.
  12. Garnish with fresh basil and Parmesan.
